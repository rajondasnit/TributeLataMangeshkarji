Lata Mangeshkar

nightangle of bollywood

September 28, 1929 The iconic singer was born

1942 She started her singing career by singing for a Marathi film.

1945 Moved to Mumbai.

1948 Master Vinayak, her early mentor passed away.

1950's Lata ji had become a popular playback singer in Bollywood.

1969 She won the Padma Bhushan

1993 Bestowed with Life time achievement award

1999 She was honored with the Padma Vibhushan

2001 Winner of the Bharat Ratna award

2009 Mangeshkar was honoured with France's highest order, the title of Officer of the French Legion of Honour.

2012 Mangeshkar's last song for a film was 'Jeena Hai Kya' for 'Dunno Y2... Life Is A Moment

2022 INDIA lost her Nightangle.The government declared national mourning for two days with the tricolor flying at half-mast
